turn on my vinyl

2020-09-19

Recently I have got a new hobby: vinyl collections.
Talking about vinyl, I used to think that it is quite antique, and probably no one would like to treat it as a valuable item until I realised buying vinyl nowadays is the new black.

Scrolling through webs, I find tons of delicate gadgets that are thoroughly designed by artists. And for some reason, records are painted in colors even transparent.

Sounds from my turntable are mild, sometimes dynamic, and sometimes are lo-fi (harmonic distortion). I like the vibes which can be only provided by Lps, that is
so-called 'rituals of playing music'.
